ABOUT

Ado

Ado was born on October 24, 2002, in Tokyo, Japan. She began her musical journey as an online cover singer at the age of fourteen, quickly gaining attention for her expressive and dramatic voice.

In 2020, she made her major debut with the song “Usseewa.” The track’s rebellious tone and raw emotion resonated with a young generation, turning Ado into a nationwide sensation almost overnight.

Her music blends J-Pop, rock, and Vocaloid-inspired sounds, often exploring emotions like frustration, freedom, and identity. Her songs capture the struggles of youth and self-expression. Ado is known for her incredible vocal range and her ability to switch between softness and intensity in a single breath.

Preferring to stay mysterious, she rarely shows her face, allowing her voice and art to speak for her.

In 2022, she sang as Uta in One Piece Film: Red, performing hits like “New Genesis,” which brought her global recognition. Today, Ado stands as one of Japan’s most influential young artists.
