CHAI JIA TONG

“Softness is not weakness; it is strength in disguise.” — Hayao Miyazaki

Hi, I’m Chai Jia Tong.

I’d say I’m mostly quiet, but when I care about something, I give it my full attention. I enjoy taking photos, listening to music, and sometimes writing down little thoughts.

I don’t back down easily when facing challenges, but I’m not in a rush to compare myself with others. What matters to me is growing step by step, doing the things I love, and keeping life at my own pace.

I hope that in the future, I can stay true to myself, remain gentle, and at the same time become stronger, shining with my own light.
